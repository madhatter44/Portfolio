Describing copyright and other constraints afecting websites

Copyright protects the rights of someone who creates an original image or idea, allowing the original owner the rights to reproduce the work, display and sell copies, there is fair use where you can use an image for educational or news purposes. Describing access issues that need taking into account?

People with disabilities may face issues in access web pages like people with blindness may use speech output system to read aloud text, vision loss like hazy vision,low vision or colour blindness may use large monitor or an anti glare screen as well as contrast between colours used in texts and their background.[5]
